## Aims of the project
In this project, we have partially reanalysed a proteomics paper (Liepe et al. 2016 — [1]).
We have also investigated if the identified peptides from the article could originate from a pool of non-canonical proteins.

Another aim was to check whether splicing affects HLA-binding affinity and whether it could be an adaptation that changes immunopeptidome in this way. As we did not reanalyse spliced peptides and the original paper did not include all in vitro peptides required for unbiased sampling, we decided to use data from another study published in 2024 [2]. 
For affinity estimation NetMHCpan-4.2 [4] was used.
